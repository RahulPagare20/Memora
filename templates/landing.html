About Memora

Memora was built with one simple belief that every person deserves to hold on to the people and movements that matter most, no matter what.

Dementia affects millions of families worldwide. Watching a loved one struggle to recognize the faces or recall precious memories is truly hearbreaking. Memora exists to bridge precisely that gap.

We combine gentle AI-powered tools with our warm, easy to use interface, which is designed specifically for patients and their caretakers - because technology should feel like a helping hand.
