🌿 Our Mission

About Memora

Memora was built with one simple belief — that every person deserves to hold on to the people and moments that matter most, no matter what.

Dementia and Alzheimer's affect millions of families worldwide. Watching a loved one struggle to recognise faces or recall precious memories is heartbreaking. Memora exists to bridge that gap.

We combine gentle AI-powered tools with a warm, easy-to-use interface designed specifically for patients and their caregivers — because technology should feel like a helping hand, not a hurdle.
